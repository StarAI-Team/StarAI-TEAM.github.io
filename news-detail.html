Star International Logistics Wins Supply Chain Excellence Award

We’re thrilled to announce that Star International Logistics has been awarded the Supply Chain Excellence Award 2024 (Gold) by the Institute of Supply Chain Management Zimbabwe (ISCMZ). This prestigious recognition underscores our unwavering commitment to delivering world-class logistics solutions and driving innovation in supply chain operations. The award ceremony, held on October 4, 2024, brought together industry leaders and visionaries to celebrate the backbone of global economies—supply chains. Keynote speaker Mr. Lusiyano, CEO of Bindura Nickel Corporation, captivated attendees with insights on how Japan’s “Just In Time” principles reshaped global industries. He also highlighted the transformative role artificial intelligence (AI) will play in the future of logistics—a vision that aligns closely with our mission. This milestone is a testament to the hard work, dedication, and forward-thinking leadership at Star International Logistics. It motivates us to continue raising the bar in logistics operations, ensuring excellence for our clients and partners every step of the way. We extend our gratitude to ISCMZ for this honor and to our incredible team and supporters who make these achievements possible. Together, we’re shaping the future of logistics.

Stay tuned for more updates as we continue striving for excellence!
